If a disaster comes your way, are YOU prepared?

This is Just a Replica Site, this is NOT the actual CART site.

It's important to be prepared for any kind of disaster or emergency. Most people have created a basic plan for themselves and their familes, but often forget the needs of their furry/feathery/scaly family members. When a disaster hits, it could mean you have to evacuate your home. Sometimes people need time to regroup and require a safe place to leave their animal friends temporarily. Friends and family homes are always the best choice for temporary care of your pets, but sometimes that isn't an option. That's where a county animal response team steps in to set up a temporary shelter. We can house and care for your pet until you are able to care for it again on your own.

Who are We?

Contra Costa County Animal Response Team (CoCoCART) is a team of dedicated volunteers who continuously train to set up and staff temporary animal shelters in a time of disaster or emergency. We have members of our group who specialize in all kinds of species, from cats and dogs to pigs and horses. We are constantly seeking additional training and experience so that we can provide the best care possible. Contra Costa CART functions under the guidance of Contra Costa County Animal Services Department (CCASD).
